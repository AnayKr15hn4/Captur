Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 8, 2025

1. Introduction

At Captur, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information, including photographs, in accordance with your consent and applicable privacy laws.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information from clients:

  • Full name, email address, phone number, and other contact details
  • Photographs taken during photo sessions or events
  • Event details (e.g., location, date, participants)

3. Use of Photographs

We will only use photographs of you or photographs that identify you if you provide explicit consent. Upon your consent:

  • Your images may be displayed on our website, social media channels, promotional materials, and photography galleries (online or physical)
  • Only the photographs to which you have given permission will be used
  • We will not share, sell, or use your images for any other purpose without additional consent

4. Consent for Use

Consent is obtained in writing (including digitally) through a release form, checkbox, or through communication agreement. You have the right to:

  • Choose which photos (if any) may be used
  • Revoke your consent at any time by contacting us
  • Request removal of any photos previously shared with your consent

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to ensure the security of your personal data and photographs. Access is limited to authorized personnel only.

6. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share your photographs or personal data with third parties without your explicit consent, except where required by law.

7. Your Rights

You may request to:

  • View the photographs we have on record
  • Correct or update any personal information
  • Withdraw consent or request deletion of specific images

9. Changes to This Policy
